# Deployment Notes

Deployment environment:

- Tight underwater camera housing
- Camera and electronics in constrained space
- Internal cable harnesses preferred over screw terminals

Deployment checks:

- Verify all connectors can be mated inside housing.
- Verify cable bend radius.
- Verify board mounting clearance.
- Verify camera battery/dummy-battery cable path.
- Verify motor and proximity sensor harness routing.
- Verify camera can operate from 8V DC path and external battery path.
- Verify charging behavior before field use.
